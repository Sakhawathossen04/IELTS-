## 📌 What is this Repository?

This is my **personal IELTS preparation repository** where I document my complete 8-month journey toward Band 8.5.

Every day's notes, practice files, mistakes, resources, and progress scores are tracked here.

**If you are preparing for IELTS**, this repo will help you because:
- It has a structured month-by-month roadmap
- Each section (Listening · Reading · Writing · Speaking) has dedicated resources
- Real practice materials, templates, and notes are included
- It is updated daily — you can learn alongside me

## ⚡ Top Tips Per Section

**Listening**
> After every test, read the transcript — understand exactly why each answer was wrong.

**Reading**
> Time management is everything. 20 minutes per passage is a hard limit.

**Writing**
> Spend 5 minutes planning Task 2 before you write a single sentence. It saves time overall.

**Speaking**
> Record yourself and listen back. It is the fastest way to identify and fix your own mistakes.

**Overall**
> Keep an error log. When you see patterns in your mistakes, you fix them faster.
